Welcome to the World of Forumers!

Tales of the Forumers (TOF) is the official Spin-Off series of the main saga, The Traveling Forumers (TTF). While TTF explores the grand, overarching narrative, TOF dives deep into the little-known corners of the world, offering short, themed story collections that enrich the lore.

What Makes TOF Unique?

  • Lore Deep-Dives: We focus on individual characters, unique locations, and unexplored histories mentioned briefly in the main TTF series.
